Privacy Policy

App: Nex QR & Barcode Scanner
Last updated: March 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Nex QR & Barcode Scanner does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The app uses your device's camera solely to scan QR codes and barcodes. Camera images are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded to any server.

2. Camera Permission

The app requests access to your camera to provide its core functionality: scanning QR codes and barcodes. The camera feed is processed locally in real time and is never saved, recorded, or transmitted.

3. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage data such as the number of active users, device type, and country. This data does not include any personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve the app.

For more information on how Google processes this data, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Scan History

Scan history is stored locally on your device only using a local database. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to external servers.

5. Third-Party Libraries

The app uses the following third-party libraries:

  • Google ML Kit – for on-device barcode scanning
  • Firebase Analytics – for anonymous usage statistics
  • CameraX – for camera access

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page.
